Q: Why am I getting an invalid image message?
A: Sheet Music Analyzer accepts image formats such as .png, .jpeg, and .heic. If you are uploading an image of that type and still getting error messages, the file is likely corrupted.

Q: What is LilyPond?
A: LilyPond is a text based software that generates sheet music in .pdf, .png, or .midi format. Read more about LilyPond at lilypond.org
